Lifetime Tamarack Angler Kayak Fishing for Bass and Crappie

Mark E. Chiles March 7, 2019 fishing

Join me on my maiden voyage with my new Lifetime Tamarack Angler 100 Kayak. I go out to a nice, calm place just off of Spring Creek. I'm a novice and you can tell. But, I don't get skunked! Pull in my personal best white crappie and a largemouth. There will be many more adventures on the kayak.
