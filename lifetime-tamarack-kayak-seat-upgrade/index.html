Lifetime Tamarack Kayak Seat Upgrade

Mark E. Chiles May 6, 2019 diy kayak seat

I have started doing tournaments and the seat needed some work. I do a kayak seat modification and upgrade by building a wooden cradle for my new stadium seat on my Lifetime Tamarack Angler kayak. Puts me up off of the boat better, allows me to see further and it's just all-around more comfortable.
