Fattest Bullhead Ever! Huge Multi-Species Day on the Kayak! | Lake Holcomb Crappie, Bass and Catfish

Mark E. Chiles March 21, 2019 bass

I take the kayak out to Lake Holcomb and catch a TON of fish! Literally the fattest bullhead catfish I have EVER seen! It is a great trip with a great outcome. I get a bit rained on....ok it pours on me, but that's fishing. Come along and see all of the action and the catches! This is what kayak fishing is all about!
